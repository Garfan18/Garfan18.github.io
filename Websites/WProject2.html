Digital Publikation | Adrian Frutiger

A Digital Publikation for the typographer Adrian Frutiger

Project Information

  • Project Type: Digital publication
  • Date: 2022
  • Used Programs: Adobe XD, Adobe InDesign

Overview

This project was created as a module project, where the task was to create a digital application for a well-known typographer in Adobe XD for use on a tablet. I chose Adrian Frutiger, a renowned typographer from Switzerland, for this project. For the project, it was important for me to showcase the most famous typographies of Frutiger and incorporate a page that tells his story. I also used his own typefaces for the work. I created numerous design prototypes before arriving at this final result.

Technical Details

  • Engine: Adobe XD
  • Resolution: IPad
  • Platform: IPad

Challenges

The challenge was to avoid overdesigning the whole thing and to bring out the fonts that had been created.
