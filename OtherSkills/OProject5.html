Environment Concept Art

Learning the basics of environment concept art

Project Information

  • Project Type: Environment Concept Art
  • Date: 2024
  • Used Programs: Clip Studio Paint

Overview

Concept art is a field that I find particularly inspiring. Although I had enjoyed drawing, I had not previously engaged with concept art. I therefore decided to undertake a mentorship with Colton Dubell, with the aim of acquiring the fundamentals of concept art using environment art. During the week-long meetings, I completed various homework assignments, and the end result was very satisfactory.

Technical Details

  • Engine: Clip Studio Paint
  • Device: IPad
  • Resolution: 5000x5000

Challenges

Keeping track of all the new tips and tricks I've learned and using them while drawing.
