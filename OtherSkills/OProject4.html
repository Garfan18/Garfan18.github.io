Cosplay prop

Creating digital characters prop in real life

Project Information

  • Project Type: Physical prototyping
  • Date: 2023
  • Used Programs: Cinema4D, Arduino

Overview

Mentoring X is a second mentoring programme that takes place during a semester. This allowed me to undertake two mentoring programmes simultaneously. In this instance, I elected to continue exploring the subject of prototyping. In my personal time, I began working on cosplay projects. Primarily for my favourite game, Rainbow Six Siege. The character, Solis, is a new addition to the game. The character's distinctive feature is her glasses, which utilise Athenas to identify electrical devices. I constructed these glasses to scale. I modelled and tested the glasses with Adruinos and micro motors, enabling the two Athenas to be raised and lowered at the touch of a button. I paid close attention to various factors, including weight, stability and construction. The glasses were printed using a powder 3D printer and assembled to ensure a successful end result.

Technical Details

  • Engine: Cinema4D
  • Language: C++
  • 3D Printer: Nylondruck

Challenges

Keep the entire model as close to the original as possible, leaving enough space for the mechanics.
