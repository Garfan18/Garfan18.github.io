V-Tuber

My personal V-Tuber

Project Information

  • Project Type: V-Tuber
  • Date: 2022
  • Used Programs: Clip Studio Paint, Live2D

Overview

The creation of this project coincided with the surge in popularity of V-Tubers within the streaming industry. Given my own experience in streaming, I was enthusiastic about creating my own V-Tuber as a mentoring project.My decision to base my V-Tuber on my pride in being in the fire brigade stemmed from my desire to portray a strong character who embodies fearlessness. I also wanted it to look a bit like me so that it didn't feel strange to use it for streaming. Unfortunately, I never got to use him because I had to take a break from streaming to concentrate on my studies.

Technical Details

  • Engine: Live2D

Challenges

Draw each part on its own layer and choose the correct order for them.
