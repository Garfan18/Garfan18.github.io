Physical Prototyping

A combination of coding and physical mechanics

Project Information

  • Project Type: Physical Prototyping
  • Date: 2023
  • Used Programs: Illustrator, Arduino

Overview

In order to facilitate a more profound comprehension of the significance of prototype creation for our projects, a dedicated module was incorporated into the curriculum. This module encompassed the process of physical prototyping. The objective was to engineer a machine capable of producing postcards utilising Arduinos, motors and MDF boards. It was quickly apparent that the initial versions of a project often contain significant flaws and require refinement through prototyping. The final project involved constructing a machine that utilizes marbles of various colours, allowing them to roll over the postcard by moving it in a lateral direction.

Technical Details

  • Engine: Arduino
  • Language: C++

Challenges

Creating the whole machine and make it work.
