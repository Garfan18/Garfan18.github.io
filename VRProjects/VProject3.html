Perception of Hesitation

Bachelor project

Project Information

  • Project Type: VR Experience
  • Date: 2025
  • Used Programs: Unity
  • Role: Solo bachelor project

Overview

This is a VR experience in which users walk through four rooms. Each room plays with focus and periphery in its own way, evoking emotions that are unusual for VR. The first room introduces users to what they can expect. In the second room, users become the observed figure. In the third, they lose sight of their goal. In the fourth room, the focus reveals a different world to that seen in the periphery. Together, these rooms create a multi-layered game of perception and expectation.
