VR Feasibility study

A feasibility study for the VR Game Gray Senses

Project Information

  • Project Type: VR Feasibility study
  • Date: 2023
  • Used Programs: Unity, Google Forms
  • Role: Solo project

Overview

The last project I did was a feasibility study for a game concept I have. For the first time I created a VR room with Unity for an Oculus Quest 2. First of all I wanted to find out if it was comfortable for the players to see and play with only one eye in greyscale and with the other eye in colour. I also tested two mechanics for closing one eye. With the first mechanic, one eye turned black when the controller was held in front of said eye, thus closing it. With the second, the eye then changed its view to greyscale or colour. I used a tutorial from Unity as the basis for the VR room and then tweaked it to suit my needs. When it was finished, I had twelve people test it and fill out a questionnaire. The result was very positive, but I I plan on continuing to collect opinions especially after people play the game for longer. This will also be my next step in implementing the game concept.
